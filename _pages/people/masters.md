## Master's Students

Several of my graduated students (Master's) have first-author papers at ACL, ICLR, and Q1 journals. They include **Garry Kuwanto** (Boston University, 2026) — *MetaMetrics*, a Bayesian optimization framework for calibrating LLM evaluation metrics (ICLR 2025); **Hairurahman** (Monash, 2025) — a hybrid knowledge graph for Indonesian sentencing analysis (Artificial Intelligence and Law, 2026); **Adhi Pranawiyana** (Monash, 2024) — predicting LLM correctness in prosthodontics using metadata and hallucination signals (HealthInf 2026); **Iwan Darmawan** (Monash, 2024) — a corpus for the Javanese honorific system (ACL 2025); and **Fariz Akyas** (Monash, 2024) — annotator identity in an Indonesian political polarization corpus (ACL Findings 2025).

Current Master's students in the lab at Monash University, Indonesia include Cindy Wanady, Carina Utomo, Rizky Nur, Iqbal Alhadi, and Hesti Rahmawati.
