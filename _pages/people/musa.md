**Musa Wijanarko** is a research assistant (Bachelor in Mathematics, ITB, 2021) in the lab working on datasets and benchmarks for Indonesian NLP, with a focus on toxicity and online harms. He is a contributing author on *NusaAksara: A Multimodal and Multilingual Benchmark for Preserving Indonesian Indigenous Scripts* (ACL 2025), *A Multi-Labeled Dataset for Indonesian Discourse: Toxicity, Polarization, and Demographics* (ACL Findings 2025), *IndoToxic2024: A Demographically-Enriched Dataset of Hate Speech and Toxicity Types*, and *Monitoring Hate Speech in Indonesia* (EMNLP 2024 Demo).
